Tamago Kake Gohan

About the Dish

Tamago kake gohan is a dish or way of eating rice with a raw egg which is drizzled with a condiment such as soy sauce or mentsuyu.
It can also feature various toppings to suit your tastes. It's typically eaten for breakfast but can also be enjoyed for lunch or brunch. These days, some call it 'TKG', an acronym for its Romanized form.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot cooked white rice
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon mirin
  • Pinch kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Cook white rice
  2. Place rice in a bowl and make a shallow indentation in the center
  3. Break the whole egg into the center
  4. Season with 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce, 1/2 teaspoon mirin
  5. a pinch of salt, a pinch of MSG (if using)
